Home

Welcome to OpenRow! Created by Class of 2016 seniors Jo Getz, Katie-Rose Skelly, Sabelle Smythe, and Gus Woythaler, this web application aims to help all Row residents visualize house spending in a simple and elegant way. Financial Managers can upload their weekly spreadsheets (in the template format given by ResEd) to keep their residents up-to-date on house expenses.
